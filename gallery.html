SEDS VIT-AP

Out-Reach Event

The SEDS Aurora club organized an outreach event at Gandhi Municipal Corporation High School, 1 Town, Vijayawada, to spark students’ interest in space. Through engaging PowerPoint presentations, interactive quizzes, and telescope sessions, we introduced fundamental space concepts and encouraged curiosity about the cosmos. Students explored celestial objects firsthand, making learning both fun and educational. By fostering a passion for space exploration, we hope to empower the next generation of scientists and innovators.

 1  Event Title  Black Holes
 2  About  The message of this lecture is that black holes ain’t as black as they are painted. They are not the eternal prisons they were once though…things can get out of a black hole both on the outside and possibly to another universe.
