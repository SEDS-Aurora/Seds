About Us



SEDS Aurora or SEDS VIT-AP is a part of SEDS India, a student based organization which promotes the exploration and development of space. SEDS is working at the forefront of making Space Exploration a popular hobby and develop students interest in the field as a viable future career option. SEDS provides an organized pathway for students to begin space related projects and give them a chance to break away from just the usual class-work. SEDS also helps as a stepping stone to a space-related career. We at SEDS India have three divisions working in three directions: Core, Outreach and Projects. Our Core Team which believes in the mantra of 'learning' has been organizing various guest lectures and activities to increase the knowledge of the students interested in the infinite possibilities of space. Our Outreach team is also working very hard to bring in sponsorships for the project, and also collaborating with other SEDS chapters and clubs all around the nation. Our Project team has been working extremely hard to create a state-of-the-art Rover and also a few other projects.
